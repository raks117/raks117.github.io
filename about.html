Our Story

Since the 1990s, S K Plastic Works has been transforming plastic waste into high-quality granules — serving industries across India with trust, consistency, and a commitment to the environment.

Who We Are

From Humble Beginnings to a Trusted Name in Plastic Recycling

S K Plastic Works was founded in the 1990s with a single, clear purpose: to give discarded plastic a second life. Starting with a modest setup, we have grown over three decades into a well-respected manufacturer and supplier of recycled HDPE and PP granules in India.

What began as a small reprocessing unit has expanded into a full-scale facility equipped with industrial washing plants and high-output extruders — all operated by a skilled team that takes pride in delivering consistent quality with every batch.

♻️

"We don't just process plastic — we believe that responsible recycling is the foundation of a sustainable industrial economy in India."

Today, we supply HDPE and PP granules to manufacturers, converters, and traders across India, building long-term relationships founded on reliability, fair pricing, and uncompromising quality.

Our Facility

State-of-the-Art Recycling Infrastructure

Our plant is equipped with modern machinery to ensure clean, efficient, and high-volume plastic reprocessing from scrap to granule.

Plastic Washing Plant

Our high-capacity washing plant removes dirt, labels, and contaminants from plastic scrap, ensuring the raw material is thoroughly clean before entering the extrusion line.

Extrusion Plant

Our extruders melt, filter, and homogenise cleaned plastic at precise temperatures, forming uniform strands that are water-cooled and cut into consistent granules.

Quality & Packaging

Each batch of granules is tested for melt flow index and visual quality before being packed in 25 kg or 50 kg bags, ready for dispatch across India.
