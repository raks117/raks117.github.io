  • Color: Natural / Black / Mix
  • Form: Granules / Pellets

How We Work

Our Recycling Process

From raw plastic scrap to premium-grade granules — a clean, efficient, and responsible 4-step process.

♻️
Step 01

Collection & Sourcing

We source post-industrial and post-consumer HDPE & PP plastic scrap from verified, reliable suppliers across India.

→
🚿
Step 02

Washing & Sorting

Industrial washing plants thoroughly clean the scrap. Material is sorted by plastic type, grade, and quality.

→
⚙️
Step 03

Extrusion

State-of-the-art extruders melt and homogenise the clean plastic, forming uniform strands that are cut into granules.

→
📦
Step 04

Quality Check & Dispatch

Every batch is inspected for MFI, density, and visual quality before being packed and dispatched to clients.
